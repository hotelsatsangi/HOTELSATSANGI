About Us

HOTEL SATSANGI is a family-run haven established to provide comfortable and affordable stays for pilgrims and travelers visiting the serene Satsang Deoghar area. Located near Bor Da Bari, we have been welcoming guests for years, offering a peaceful escape blended with modern amenities and traditional hospitality.

Our mission is to ensure every guest feels at home, with clean rooms, warm service, and easy access to spiritual sites. We prioritize value, cleanliness, and guest satisfaction in every aspect of our service.
